the strongest factor shaping the educational landscape

Another current trend in education is the blending of STEM/STEAM with AI and other immersive Technologies. According to Hachey et al (2022), “Technology is perhaps the strongest factor shaping the educational landscape today.”

Young children are in a constant state of exploring, learning, and engaging with their surroundings as they grow. Hands-on materials such as paper, crayons, safety scissors, blocks, and sensory materials are among the many items they use as they explore and discover. Using technology in classroom settings can also widen their field and help stretch their imagination.

Children not only become familiar with and develop their computer skills, but the use of videos of them at play, or pictures of their artwork and engagement activities, is one way to generate interest and celebrate their successes (Technology and Young Children: Preschoolers and Kindergartners, 2026).

This integration will help foster creativity and growth in mathematical and scientific structures, as well as other areas of learning.
