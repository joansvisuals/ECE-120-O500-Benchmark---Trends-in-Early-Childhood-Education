Efforts spurred by these trends have led to a renewed focus on what are now known as school-wide initiatives. Schoolwide initiatives are measures being taken to establish and expand social-emotional programs that foster holistic wellness in young children.

Activities such as the kindness challenge, which recognizes children for acts of kindness toward their peers and others, help teach children empathy and respect for others. There is also a strong emphasis on regular attendance, with students recognized for perfect attendance.

Young children are also being taught civic and environmental awareness through recycling drives, school garden planting, and resource conservation. Community involvement is also encouraged with mentorship programs and family and parent involvement.

Using these school-ready, age-appropriate initiatives reinforces holistic wellness, builds self-awareness, and engenders civic responsibility, which can be strengthened as they grow.
